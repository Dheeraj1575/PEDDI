The Story

Set in the 1980s rural Andhra Pradesh, PEDDI follows the inspiring journey of a spirited villager who becomes a symbol of hope and unity for his entire community. In a land where power corrupts and the mighty oppress the weak, one man dares to challenge the status quo.

When his village faces the threat of a powerful rival determined to crush their spirit and seize their pride, PEDDI rises to the occasion. Armed with nothing but determination and the will of his people, he unites the entire community through the universal language of sports. What begins as a fight for survival transforms into a powerful movement of resilience, dignity, and collective strength.

Through sweat, sacrifice, and unwavering spirit, PEDDI leads his village to reclaim their honor and stand against all odds. A saga of raw power, broken boundaries, and an unbreakable spirit — celebrating the victory of the underdog and the triumph of unity over tyranny.

A Pan-India epic that transcends borders — in language, in scale, and in heart. When destiny calls, PEDDI answers.
